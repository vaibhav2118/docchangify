Frequently Asked Questions

Are my images safe? Does this upload to a server?

Yes, your images are 100% safe. This application is completely "Client-Side" - meaning the file conversion happens inside your own computer processor and browser memory. No files are ever sent over the internet.

What happens to transparent pixels in PNG?

The JPG format does not support transparency. When we convert your PNG, any transparent pixels are automatically replaced with a clean, solid white background.

Why convert PNG to JPG?

PNG files can be very large because they are largely uncompressed. JPG uses lossy compression, which significantly reduces the file size (often by 70% or more), saving huge amounts of space on your hard drive and loading much faster on websites.
